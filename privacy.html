Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2026

San Diego Home Buyers ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website sandiegohomebuyers.org. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and applicable federal law.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website or submit a form:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Property address or search preferences
  • Financial information provided voluntarily (income, debt, down payment estimate)
  • IP address, browser type, and usage data via cookies and analytics tools

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and connect you with a licensed mortgage or real estate professional
  • To process your pre-application or pre-qualification request
  • To send follow-up communications related to your home search or loan inquiry
  • To improve our website and marketing efforts
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Licensed mortgage professionals and real estate agents assisting with your transaction
  • Third-party service providers who help operate our website (e.g., Google Analytics, Google Ads)
  • Regulatory authorities when required by law

4. Google Analytics & Advertising

We use Google Analytics and Google Ads to understand how visitors use our site and to serve relevant advertisements. Google may use cookies to track your activity. You can opt out of Google Analytics by visiting tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@sandiegohomebuyers.org.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
